How to Get Featured in Google AI Overviews — A Step-by-Step 2026 Guide

Jan 5, 20267 min read

AI Overviews are reshaping organic click-through rates in 2026. Google's AI-powered search results now appear at the top of many queries, fundamentally changing how users interact with search. Here's how to optimise your content to be featured.

What Are Google AI Overviews?

AI Overviews are AI-generated summaries that appear at the top of Google search results. They synthesize information from multiple sources to provide direct answers, reducing the need for users to click through to individual websites.

Why Being Featured Matters

While AI Overviews may reduce overall clicks, being cited as a source provides significant benefits:

  • Brand visibility - Your site is mentioned as a trusted source
  • Authority building - Google considers your content high-quality
  • Targeted traffic - Users who do click are highly qualified
  • Competitive advantage - Most businesses aren't optimizing for this yet

How to Optimize for AI Overviews

1. Create Comprehensive, Authoritative Content

AI Overviews pull from sources that thoroughly cover topics. Write in-depth content that answers related questions comprehensively. Aim for 2,000+ words on important topics.

2. Use Clear, Direct Language

Write in a clear, straightforward manner. Avoid jargon unless necessary. Use short paragraphs and bullet points to make information easily digestible.

3. Structure Content with Headers

Use H2 and H3 headers to break content into clear sections. This helps AI understand your content structure and extract relevant information.

4. Answer Specific Questions

Include FAQ sections that directly answer common questions. Use question-based headers ("What is...", "How to...", "Why does...")

5. Build Topical Authority

Cover topics comprehensively across multiple related articles. Google is more likely to cite sources that demonstrate expertise across an entire subject area.

6. Include Data and Statistics

AI Overviews often cite specific data points. Include original research, statistics, and credible sources in your content.

7. Optimize for E-E-A-T

Demonstrate Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness. Include author bios, credentials, and cite authoritative sources.

The Future of Search

AI Overviews represent a fundamental shift in search. While they may reduce traditional organic traffic, they also create new opportunities for visibility. The key is adapting your SEO strategy to focus on being a cited source rather than just ranking in position one.

Start implementing these strategies now to stay ahead as AI continues to transform search in 2026 and beyond.
